Samples for Semantic Blending Experiment

Introduction

We have selected two random 5 second segments A and B from each domain and embedded both using the encoder, obtaining $e_A$ and $e_B$. Then, we combine the embeddings as follows:
starting with 3.5 second from $e_A$, we combine the next 1.5 seconds of $e_B$ with the first 1.5 seconds of $e_B$ using a linear weighting with weights $1 - t/1.5$ and $t/1.5$ respectively, where $t \in [0,1.5]$.
We then use the decoder to generate audio. The results are natural and the shift is completely seamless, as seen below.
